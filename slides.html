R Code

library(readr)
library(dplyr)

labdata <- readr::read_csv("data/labdata.csv")

group_by(labdata, Measure) %>% 
    summarise(min = min(Result),
              mean = mean(Result),
              sd = sd(Result))

Demo 1

\[Q = \frac{2}{3} C_d \sqrt{2g} \; bh^\frac{3}{2}\]

  • \(Q\): Discharge
  • \(C_d\): Discharge coefficient
  • \(g\): Acceleration of gravity
  • \(b\): Crest length
  • \(h\): Head above crest level

  • \(b=0.5\) m
  • \(h=0.1\) m
  • \(C_d=0.6\)
  • \(g=9.81\) m/s2
